Property Details

back to the top

The Wilson is truly unlike any apartment community. Housed in the circa 1904 J.B. Wilson Building which was was modeled on Paris' Grand Opera House. Over the years it has been a department store and office building. Today, it is home to 135 of Dallas' most authentic apartments and penthouses.
