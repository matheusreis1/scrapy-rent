Property Details

The View Apartments are beautifully maintained, affordable apartment homes that "Sit Above the Rest". The clean and peaceful surroundings will make you never want to leave your private patio/balcony. Located on the ridge near Shelby Medical Center, convenient to Alabaster Schools, parks, restaurants, and the Promenade Shopping Center. Boasting a private pool area, spectacular views of the sunset and surrounded by beautiful natural landscape, you will want to call The View your new home!
